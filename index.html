Big Data Analytics
Big data analytics examines huge and diverse big data sets that include structured, semi-structured, or unstructured data, from different sources, or in varying sizes from terabytes to zettabytes to uncover hidden patterns, correlations, and other insights using advanced analytic techniques. With today’s technology, it’s possible to analyze your data and get answers from it almost immediately when compared to slower and less efficient traditional business intelligence solutions.
